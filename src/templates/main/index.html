Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our memberships and facilities.

How do I register at the club?

Registration is simple - fill out the online form on our website or visit our center in person. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to help you choose the most suitable program.

What is the minimum age to train?

Our center accepts members from 14 years old. For teenagers aged 14-18, parental consent is required. We have specially designed safe programs for all ages.

Can I get a personalized training program?

Yes, our certified instructors will develop a fully personalized workout plan taking into account your goals, physical preparation, and health condition.

How does payment work and what are the prices?

Payment can be made in cash, by card on-site, or via online transfer. Prices start from 40₾ per month and depend on the chosen program. We also offer student and family discounts.

Can I transfer my membership to another person?

No, for security reasons, memberships are strictly personal and cannot be transferred to anyone else. Progress and health metrics are tracked individually for each member.

Can I bring a guest?

Yes, permanent members can bring a guest for free once a month. The guest must complete a health questionnaire and sign a safety declaration before training.

What programs are recommended for beginners?

For beginners, we recommend Yoga, Pilates, General Fitness, and Swimming. We offer a free consultation with a trainer for all new members, along with supervision for the first 3 sessions.
