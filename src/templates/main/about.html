Our Services and Programs

Diverse and innovative approaches for all ages and fitness levels

Personal Training

A fully individual approach with a certified trainer, tailored to your goals, health condition, and personal pace. The program continuously adapts based on your progress.

Group Classes

A variety of group workouts: Zumba, Pilates, Yoga, Functional Training, Strength, and Endurance. Each class is designed for different fitness levels and provides collective motivation.

Digital Tracking

Innovative technologies for precise monitoring of your progress: workout intensity, heart rate, calories burned, and overall physical form metrics. All data is available in the app.

Wellness Programs

A holistic approach to health: meditation, relaxation, outdoor activities, and stress management techniques. These programs focus on both physical and psychological well-being.

Nutrition Consultation

Certified nutritionists will develop personalized meal plans based on your goals, allergies, and preferences. We believe that proper nutrition is 70% of fitness success.

24/7 Access

Complete flexibility - the center is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. A modern security system and climate control ensure a comfortable environment at any time.
